What Are Some Common Ways Private Property is Violated?

Home burglary is the act of a person breaking or entering a property (usually a house) with the intent of committing a crime. Every 15 seconds a home burglary occurs in the United States.This means that every 15 seconds, there is a person in the USA that trespasses into someone's house. If you do that math, there are 31536000 seconds in a year, if you divide that by 15, you get 2,102,400 house burglaries in the USA a year.
